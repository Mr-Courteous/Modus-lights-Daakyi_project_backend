CYBERSECURITY POLICY DOCUMENT
TechCorp Inc. Information Security Policy
Version 2.1 - Effective Date: January 2025

1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
This policy establishes the framework for protecting TechCorp's information assets and ensuring compliance with industry standards including NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0.

2. ORGANIZATIONAL CONTEXT
TechCorp recognizes cybersecurity as a critical business enabler. Our mission includes protecting customer data, maintaining service availability, and ensuring regulatory compliance.

3. GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE
- Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) oversees cybersecurity strategy
- Security Committee meets monthly to review risk posture
- Board receives quarterly cybersecurity briefings

4. RISK MANAGEMENT APPROACH
- Annual risk assessments identify and prioritize threats
- Risk appetite statement defines acceptable risk levels
- Continuous monitoring ensures ongoing risk visibility

5. ACCESS CONTROL REQUIREMENTS
- Multi-factor authentication required for all systems
- Role-based access control implemented
- Regular access reviews conducted quarterly

6. DATA PROTECTION MEASURES
- Data classification scheme implemented
- Encryption required for data at rest and in transit
- Data retention policies enforced

7. INCIDENT RESPONSE
- 24/7 Security Operations Center monitors threats
- Incident response team activated for security events
- Post-incident reviews identify improvement opportunities

8. TRAINING AND AWARENESS
- Annual cybersecurity training mandatory for all employees
- Phishing simulation exercises conducted quarterly
- Security awareness communications distributed monthly

This policy supports multiple NIST CSF 2.0 controls including organizational context (GV.OC-01), risk management strategy (GV.RM-01), access control (PR.AA-01), data security (PR.DS-01), and incident response planning (RS.RP-01).
