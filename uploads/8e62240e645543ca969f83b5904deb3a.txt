
CYBERSECURITY POLICY DOCUMENT
TechCorp Inc. Information Security Policy

1. GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE
- Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) oversees cybersecurity strategy
- Security Committee meets monthly to review risk posture

2. RISK MANAGEMENT APPROACH
- Annual risk assessments identify and prioritize threats
- Risk appetite statement defines acceptable risk levels

3. ACCESS CONTROL REQUIREMENTS
- Multi-factor authentication required for all systems
- Role-based access control implemented

4. DATA PROTECTION MEASURES
- Data classification scheme implemented
- Encryption required for data at rest and in transit

5. INCIDENT RESPONSE
- 24/7 Security Operations Center monitors threats
- Incident response team activated for security events
